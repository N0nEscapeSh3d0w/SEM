FOCS FAQ

Frequently Asked Qeustions

Q: What programmes does the Faculty of Computer Science offer for enrollment? +
A: The Faculty of Computer Science offers a variety of programmes, including diploma and degree programmes. You can explore our programme offerings on our official website.
Q: How can I enroll in a diploma programme in Computer Science at your faculty? +
A: To enroll in a diploma programme, go to our website's diploma page and select your favourite programme from the list. After that, you can fill out the form by clicking the enroll button.
Q: Can I transfer credits from another institution to your diploma program in Computer Science? +
A: Yes, we do consider credit transfers from other recognized institutions. Contact our academic advisors for more information and to evaluate your transfer credits.
Q: Where can I find the staff directory for the Faculty of Computer Science? +
A: Our staff directory is available on the staff page.
Q: What undergraduate degree programs are available at the Faculty of Computer Science? +
A: We offer a range of undergraduate degree programs in Computer Science, including Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Computer Science and related fields. You can find more information on the particular programme page.
Q: How can I get in touch with the main reception or information desk of your Faculty of Computer Science? +
A: The contact number and address of the TARUMT campus can be found at the bottom of the website page.
